Philosophy

Wisdom is obtained through seeking it out. I have enjoyed studying philosophy in my own life because it has informed me on how best to live. Philosophy is the study of the fundamental nature of knowledge, reality, and existence, especially when considered as an academic discipline. It is a subject that has been studied for thousands of years, and has been a major influence on the development of Western thought. My personal favorite philosopher is Cicero.

Favorite philosophers

Cicero
Schopenhauer
Kierkegaard

My favorite book of Cicero's is "On Living and Dying Well", which explores the nature of life and death, and how to live a good life.

Some of the most influential philosophers in history include:
  • Socrates
  • Plato
  • Aristotle
  • Descartes
  • Kant
  • Nietzsche
